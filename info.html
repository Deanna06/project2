The Amazing Lion

Let Me Hear Your Rawr!

Lions are the one of the most fascinating animals in the world. Lions are strong and powerful felines. They are usually found in Africa, Asia, and Europe. Their fur color can be a mix of a gold, blonde, or brown color. Lions can grow up to 6.5 feet long and can weigh up to 500 lbs. Their life spand is between ten and fourteen years old. Males are generally larger than females. Males mane are usually large and full. Females do not have a mane. The mane for male lions make them look more striking and intimdating to other male lions. This mane helps protects lions during a fight. The mane and its impact on lions is very impressive to female lions.

Their habitat

Lions territories consist of grasslands, savannas, and rockyhills. These places makr hunting easier for lions. In these territories, there is prey that is easy to hunt and hard to hunt. With easy prey, lions are usually fine huunting by themselves or with another lion. With fast and hard to catch prey, lions would usually hunt in a pack with more than three lions.
